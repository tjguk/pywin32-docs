win32gui.PolyBezier

PolyBezier(hdc, Points)

Draws a series of Bezier curves starting from first point specified.

Parameters

hdc : PyHANDLE

Handle to a device context

Points : [(int,int),...]

Sequence of POINT tuples: ((x,y),...).

Comments

Number of points must be a multiple of 3 plus 1.
